Syllabus of Sainik School Entrance Exam

Exam for admission is conducted for admission in VIth and IXth. The medium of the exam is English for class IXth and Hindi for Class VIth.

Subjects for Class VI

Mathematics and Language Ability Test (200 marks)

Intelligence Test (100 marks)

Subjects for Class IX

Mathematics and Science (200+75 marks)

English and Social Studies (100+75marks)
